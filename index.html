Data Practices

Information We Collect

We do not operate analytics pipelines or advertising back-ends. The following explains what data exists, where it lives, and how it is used.

Data You Enter Directly (iOS Apps)

  • Financial Payment Tracker — Account nicknames, balances, credit limits, APR, payment and charge amounts, due dates, transaction memos, and payment history you manually enter.
  • My Plant Watering Schedule — Plant names, species, locations, watering schedules, care history, optional plant photos, care notes, and an optional nickname.
  • A.R.I.A. — A first name or nickname and a selected Cyber Identity title. All learning progress is stored on your device only and is never transmitted to AegisMGK Technologies or any third party.

Data You Enter (SBCC Web Portal)

  • Business name, business type, industry, state of operation, and employee count
  • Questionnaire responses (yes / no / not sure answers across compliance categories)
  • Your email address — only if you submit a free consultation request

Web portal assessment responses are processed in real time to generate your PDF report and are not stored on our servers after your session ends. If you submit a consultation request, your email address and PDF report are transmitted to AegisMGK via encrypted email and retained per our record retention policy. See the SBCC Web Portal section below for full details.

Data Stored On Your Device (iOS Apps)

All data for all three iOS apps is stored in a Core Data database on your device using Apple's standard SQLite store. For Financial Payment Tracker, sensitive authentication credentials are additionally stored in the iOS Keychain.

Data You Optionally Sync via iCloud (iOS Apps)

All three iOS apps offer optional iCloud Sync using Apple's CloudKit framework. This is entirely your choice. When enabled, data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple. AegisMGK Technologies does not have access to your iCloud data at any time. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

App Update Check (My Plant Watering Schedule Only)

Once per 24 hours, My Plant Watering Schedule silently contacts Apple's public iTunes Lookup API to check for a newer version. This request contains only the app's bundle identifier — no personal data is transmitted.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Analytics, crash reports, or usage statistics in any product
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA) or cross-app tracking
  • Your name, email address, phone number, or location (iOS apps)
  • Your financial data, plant data, or A.R.I.A. learning progress on our servers
  • SBCC assessment responses after your web portal session ends (free tier)
  • We do not sell, rent, lease, or share your data with any third party
iOS App

Financial Payment Tracker

Financial Payment Tracker is designed with your privacy as the top priority. All financial data you enter stays on your device and, if you choose, in your own personal iCloud account. We never see, collect, or have access to any of your data.

Financial Payment Tracker

Finance • Productivity • iOS / watchOS

Data Stored On Your Device

  • Account nicknames and types (e.g. "Chase Sapphire", credit card)
  • Balances, credit limits, APR, minimum payment amounts, and due dates
  • Payment and charge amounts, transaction memos, and payment history you log manually
  • Savings goal names, categories, balances, and contribution history
  • App preferences (biometric lock setting, notification preferences)

No account numbers, login credentials, or bank connection details are ever requested or stored.

Face ID / Touch ID

You may optionally enable a PIN lock secured by Face ID or Touch ID. Biometric data is processed entirely by Apple's Secure Enclave on your device and is never accessible to the app or to us. Your PIN is stored in the iOS Keychain, encrypted at the hardware level.

Push Notifications

If you grant notification permission, the app schedules local payment reminders using Apple's UNUserNotificationCenter framework. No data leaves your device to generate notifications. You can manage notifications at any time in Settings → Notifications.

iCloud Sync & Data Deletion

If you use iCloud Sync and delete your data within the app, deletions are propagated to your iCloud account through Apple's CloudKit persistent history mechanism. To permanently delete all data before uninstalling, use the Delete All Data & Account option in app Settings.

We Do Not Collect

  • Analytics, crash reports, or usage data
  • Advertising or tracking framework data
  • Any data shared with third parties
  • Server-side data of any kind
iOS App — Financial Payment Tracker

Home Screen Widget and Apple Watch

Financial Payment Tracker includes a home screen widget and an Apple Watch companion app. These features display a summary of your upcoming payment due dates and balances.

How Data Is Shared With the Widget and Watch App

The widget and Watch app receive data through an App Group shared container — a secure, on-device storage area that allows the main app and its extensions to share data without any network communication. This data transfer happens entirely on your device and never leaves it.

What Data Is Shared

  • Account nicknames and upcoming due dates for display in the widget
  • Minimum payment amounts and balances for the payoff summary view
  • No account numbers, credentials, or sensitive identifiers are included

Widget Cache

The widget cache is stored in the App Group UserDefaults container. It is cleared automatically when you use Delete All Data & Account in app Settings, and is never synced to iCloud or transmitted anywhere.

Widget & Watch Data Stays On-Device

  • All widget and Watch data is shared via on-device App Group container only
  • No widget or Watch data is transmitted to any server
  • No widget or Watch data is accessible to AegisMGK Technologies
  • Widget cache is cleared when you delete all app data

Security

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. The following measures are in place across our products:

  • Sensitive credentials (Apple User ID, app PIN) are stored in the iOS Keychain — hardware-encrypted, device-only, and excluded from unencrypted backups
  • Core Data stores use SQLite with iOS's default file protection (NSFileProtectionCompleteUntilFirstUserAuthentication)
  • iCloud data in transit is protected by TLS and encrypted at rest by Apple
  • None of our iOS apps connect to any developer-operated server, eliminating server-side breach risk entirely
  • A.R.I.A. makes no network calls — all content is bundled locally within the app, eliminating server-side breach risk
  • The SBCC web portal is served over HTTPS (TLS 1.2/1.3) via Cloudflare with CSRF protection on all form submissions
  • Consulting client documents are stored encrypted at rest on FileVault-protected local storage and are never placed in publicly accessible locations

Data Retention (iOS Apps)

We do not retain your iOS app data. Your data exists only on your device and, optionally, in your own iCloud account. Deletion is immediate and permanent when you use the in-app deletion feature.

Web Portal

SBCC Web Portal (aegismgk.com/sbcc)

The Small Business Compliance Checker web portal is a browser-based compliance assessment tool available free of charge at aegismgk.com/sbcc. It operates differently from our iOS apps — your assessment is processed on our web server rather than on your device.

What the SBCC Web Portal Collects

  • Business profile information: Business name, type, industry, state of operation, employee count, and whether you handle payments, health data, or serve children under 13.
  • Questionnaire responses: Your yes / no / not sure answers across applicable compliance categories.
  • Your email address: Only if you click Request Free Consult from AegisMGK and submit your contact request.

How Your Data Is Used

  • Your business profile and questionnaire responses are processed server-side in real time to calculate your risk scores and generate your PDF report.
  • Your assessment data is not stored in a database and is not retained on our servers after your session ends.
  • If you submit a consultation request, your email address and PDF report are transmitted to AegisMGK via encrypted email (Zoho Mail, TLS). We use your email only to respond to your request — we do not add you to any mailing list.
  • Server logs may record your IP address, browser type, and access timestamp for security and troubleshooting purposes only. These logs are not used for analytics or advertising.

Consultation Request Data Retention

If you submit a consultation request, your email address and the attached PDF report are retained in our email archive for 3 years per our record retention policy. If you engage AegisMGK for paid consulting services, your engagement documents are retained for 3–5 years depending on document type. You may request deletion of your consultation request data at any time by contacting admin@aegismgk.com.

CSRF Protection

All SBCC web portal form submissions are protected by CSRF (Cross-Site Request Forgery) tokens, which are generated fresh for each browser session and verified server-side before any processing occurs.

No Account Required

The SBCC web portal does not require an account, login, or registration of any kind. No cookies are set for tracking or advertising purposes. The only cookie used is a session cookie required for CSRF security, which expires when you close your browser.
